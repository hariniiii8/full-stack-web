HISTORY OF CLUBS AT BMS

BMSCE campus life is vibrant, and all enrolled students, day scholars and boarders witness the lively culmination of the cultures and community activities. The curriculum here promotes all-round development and ensures student wellness both inside the classrooms and outside. The varsity has innumerable clubs consisting of 25 skill enhancement clubs .

  • Chance to build your skillset.
  • Can lead to great friendships and new networking opportunities
  • Helps you become more collaborative
  • Can build your self-confidence

One the best universities in India, BMSCE offers students a plethora of club and student society activities. Irrespective of your stream or interest, there is something on campus that will interest you.
